FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

HOW TO START USING A GLOB WALLET?+

To work with the online GLOB WALLET, you must have the CHROME or FIREFOX browser installed, along with the METAMASK extension linked to an active Ethereum wallet.

First of all, you need to log in to METAMASK with a password and select the Ethereum account that you plan to use.

You can add an existing Ethereum account or create a new one.

Open your GLOB WALLET, connect to Ethereum through METAMASK and create a reserve fund.

Please note that in order to register using an Ethereum account there must be a minimum amount of ether for the transaction payment ~ 0.001ETH.

HOW TO REGISTER A RESERVE FUND?+

In order to register a reserve fund, you need to log in to GLOB WALLET using METAMASK and click on the CREATE NEW RESERVE FUND button in the pop-up notification or in the MY RESERVE tab.

If you have an invite, fill it in instead of the address (default invitation) in the field above the button. After clicking the button, confirm the transaction in the wallet and METAMASK pop-up windows.

HOW TO SEND GLOB TO ANOTHER WALLET?+

To send GLOB via the PoT protocol, go to the SEND GLOB tab, enter the required amount and the Ethereum address of the recipient's account, then click on the SEND GLOB button and confirm the transaction in the wallet and METAMASK pop-up windows. After 365 days, you will receive a 100% cashback, and the recipient will receive a 100% reward.

To send GLOB to cryptocurrency exchange accounts or smart contracts without paying for mining, use the direct ERC20 transfer by choosing ERC20 TRANSFER in the SEND GLOB tab. Please note that cashbacks and rewards are not awarded for direct ERC20 GLOB transfers.

HOW TO CREATE AN INVOICE TO RECEIVE GLOB?+

To create an invoice, go to the CREATE BILL tab, enter the amount of the invoice in GLOB, taking into account the 20% mining cost, click on the CREATE BILL button, copy the created payment link and send it to the payer.

ARE THERE TRANSACTION COMMISSIONS?+

When sending GLOB via PoT, the mining cost is 20% of the amount. This is embedded in the system's economic model to limit uncontrolled emissions and stabilize GLOB cryptocurrency liquidity. The withheld commission is fully offset by the 100% cashback.

When sending GLOB directly via the ERC20 protocol, there are no mining costs.

Since GLOB cryptocurrency was created on the Ethereum blockchain, all transaction requires a gas payment of ~ 0.001ETH.
WHERE ARE MINING PAYMENTS DIRECTED?+

When confirming the transaction, you are invited to use the checkboxes to choose how the mining payment will be used. You can send this amount to the system’s charity fund, to the developer support fund, or simply destroy it (default option).

WHAT IS A RESERVE TRANSACTION?+

A digital asset created during a PoT transaction that mines and distributes GLOB cryptocurrency. You can see your reserve transactions under MY RESERVE tab in the RESERVE TRANSACTIONS table.

HOW IS GLOB CRYPTOCURRENCY MINED?+

For mining purposes, it is sufficient to make settlements in GLOB using the PoT protocol, perform Total Mining, distribute airdrop and invite new sponsors.

When sending GLOB, you receive 100% of the amount sent as a cashback, and when you receive GLOB, you get 100% of the amount received.

When engaging in Total Mining, you receive 900% of the transaction amount in GLOB.

When sending airdrop, you get 172% and get an instant return of 64% of the airdrop cost.

When transfers are made by sponsor funds, you get 10% of the amount of their transactions, and when sponsors engage in Total Mining, you get 50% along with instant 20% bonuses from the first and 5% from the second level.

GLOB cryptocurrency mining is carried out continuously for 365 days from the date of transaction. In the beginning, the coin mining rate is minimal, then it increases exponentially, reaching its maximum value on day 365, after which the mining ends.
WHAT IS TOTAL MINING?+

A transaction with a maximum mining income of 900% of the transaction amount within 365 days for your fund.

When your 1st and 2nd-level sponsors engage in Total Mining, you get 50% of the transaction amount along with instant bonuses of 20% and 5% from each level.

HOW TO GET A CASHBACK AND MINING REWARDS?+

Go to MY RESERVE tab, select the desired transaction in the RESERVE TRANSACTIONS table, go to the transaction page, click on the GET REWARD button and confirm the transaction in the wallet and METAMASK pop-up windows.

Please note that if mining has not been completed, you will receive only that part of the amount that the transaction managed to mine, and transaction mining will end.

HOW TO VERIFY AND CONFIRM THE TRANSACTION?+

All transactions are stored in the Ethereum blockchain smart contract database and are accessible online via direct links. To get the link, go to the MY RESERVE tab, select the transaction in the RESERVE TRANSACTIONS table, and go to its page.

WHY HASN’T THE TRANSACTION APPEARED AFTER GLOB TRANSFER?+

Perhaps the transaction has not yet been completed in the Ethereum network, please wait several minutes and update the information using the REFRESH button. If the transaction still has not been completed, refresh the data again a few minutes later.

The transaction speed in the Ethereum network depends on the price of gas: the more gas is used, the faster the transaction is completed. If your transactions take too long, increase the amount of gas through Metamask.

HOW TO INVITE SPONSORS?+

The most convincing reason to invite sponsors is the mutually beneficial TOTAL MINING offer, in which your sponsor earns 900% of the transaction amount in 365 days, and you earn 50% and get an instant bonus of 20%.

To invite a sponsor, you need to give him an invitation – your fund’s Ethereum account address. The user must register a reserve fund using your invite in order to become your sponsor.

To transfer an invitation, you can use the direct link, which is located under the SPONSORS tab. Using cookies, it will automatically fill your invitation in the sponsor fund registration form.

The most effective invitation transfer tool is the distribution of free airdrop. Upon receipt of the airdrop, the sponsors’ reserve funds are automatically registered with your invitation, and the use of the airdrop pool allows to fully automate the airdrop distribution process.

SENDING AIRDROP: TO WHOM AND WHY?+

You can send airdrop only to Ethereum blockchain users who are not registered in the system and automatically create reserve funds for your sponsors.

The transaction is automatically created for the airdrop recipient, slated to extract 160% of the airdrop amount in 365 days, and you get an instant return of 64% and 172% within 365 days.

WHAT IS THE PURPOSE OF THE AIRDROP POOL?+

AIRDROP POOL is required to automate the distribution of airdrop. If you have an airdrop pool, any user will be able to independently receive your airdrop via a direct link (located in the NETWORK tab).

To create an airdrop pool, go to the AIRDROP tab, press CREATE AIRDROP POOL and fill in all the form fields. To stop airdrop distribution and return the GLOB balance to your account, press the STOP AIRDROP POOL button.

WHAT IS THE G.R.S. FOUNDATION?+

G.R.S. FOUNDATION is a charity fund of the Global Reserve System, which is formed during the work of the PoT mining stream from the funds held during transactions.

The fund's resources are allocated to finance socially significant projects in the fields of science, culture, education and health care through the Global Reserve Fund community vote.

WHY CAN’T I LOGIN TO MY GLOB WALLET?+

If you cannot log into your GLOB WALLET, make sure that:

1. You are using the CHROME or FIREFOX browser

2. The METAMASK extension is installed and the password for the METAMASK wallet is set.

3. Main Ethereum Network is selected in the METAMASK extension.

4. The METAMASK extension is active, you entered the correct password and selected the relevant Ethereum account.

5. There are no third-party extensions or ad blockers in the browser that may interfere with the GLOB WALLET application.

6. There are no active antiviruses or firewalls on your computer restricting your Internet connection.

7. Your computer is not infected with viruses or malware.

You may not be able to login to GLOB WALLET if at least one item from the list has not been completed.
